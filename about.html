About me

My name is Mads Jelager, im a 27 year old multi media student from Denmark. I go by the name ”Graaxi” online, and since i’ve spent most my teen years behind a monitor playing competitive video games, that name has become a part of my identity.

Ever since I was a little kid, ive always been elevated towards challenges and competition, and some years back I decided to take up the challenge to learn how to edit videos. The great part about learning to edit was I could combine it with my passion for video games.

Eventually I also picked up some photo editing skills and some experience using the 3d program Blender. Now as I am studying multi media design, more skillsets are getting added to my toolbox, and I’m at a point where I don’t just want to limit myself to the video gaming space.
